ABOUT

BIO.TXT

I am a geologist and earth scientist with a PhD, specializing in Precambrian and early Paleozoic geology of the East European Platform.

My research focuses on the late Ediacaran and early Cambrian sedimentary successions of southwestern Baltica — decoding the ancient passive margin through sedimentology, stratigraphy, and geochemistry.

Published in Precambrian Research and other Elsevier journals. Fieldwork across the Volyn–Podolia region and related outcrops of the EEP.

LITHOSTRATIGRAPHY & SEDIMENTOLOGY

Detailed logging and facies analysis of the Vendian–Cambrian succession, reconstructing depositional environments along the EEP passive margin.

CHEMOSTRATIGRAPHY

Carbon and strontium isotope stratigraphy across the Ediacaran–Cambrian boundary, correlating excursions with global events and extinction horizons.

PALEOENVIRONMENTAL RECONSTRUCTION

Integrating trace fossils, body fossils, and geochemical proxies to reconstruct paleo-oceanographic conditions during the Cambrian Explosion.

BIOSTRATIGRAPHY & CORRELATION

Calibrating the Vendian–Cambrian succession of the EEP against global Ediacaran fossil assemblages to constrain boundary ages.
